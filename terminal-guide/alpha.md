# Alpha

### KOL Streams

KOL Streams track activity from a curated set of **400+ influential wallets** — traders who consistently move early and shape narratives.

Instead of guessing what’s trending, you see **where attention and capital are forming**. When multiple KOLs align, it often signals the start of momentum — giving you a chance to position before the crowd.

### Smart Wallet Streams

Smart Wallets are Trench’s **proprietary selection of high-signal wallets**, dynamically ranked based on performance.

These wallets are continuously evaluated using **win rate, PnL, consistency, and early entry behavior**, ensuring you’re always tracking wallets that are **actually performing now — not historically**.
