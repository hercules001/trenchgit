# Referrals

### Referral Structure

Trench uses a **multi-level referral system** that rewards users for bringing in active traders.

* **Level 1:** 30% of trading fees
* **Level 2:** 3% of trading fees
* **Level 3:** 2% of trading fees

{% hint style="info" icon="keyboard-brightness" %}
Referred users **skip Tier I and start directly at Tier II**, effectively **doubling their cashback from the start**, making trading more efficient for them while you earn from their activity.
{% endhint %}

> **Note:** Rewards are calculated based on **trading fees only** (excluding gas and taxes).
