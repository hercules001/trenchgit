# Tiers & Cashbacks

Trench uses a **volume-based tier system** designed to reward active traders fairly and transparently.

As your trading volume increases, you move up tiers and unlock **higher cashback on fees**, reducing your effective trading costs over time.

Unlike systems that are complex or restrictive, Trench keeps it simple — **the more you trade, the more you earn back**.
