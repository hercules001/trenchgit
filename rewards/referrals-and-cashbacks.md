# Referrals & Cashbacks

## Cashback

Trench rewards active traders with a **flat 35% cashback** on trading fees from day one.

There are no tiers, unlocks, or volume requirements - every user receives the same cashback rate.

Cashback applies to both **Spot** and **Perpetual** trading.

***

## Referral Structure

Trench uses a multi-level referral system that rewards users for bringing active traders to the platform.

* **Level 1:** 30%
* **Level 2:** 3%
* **Level 3:** 2%

Referral rewards are calculated from Trench trading fees only and do not include network fees, priority fees, launchpad taxes, or exchange fees.

The referral structure is identical across **Spot** and **Perpetual** trading.
