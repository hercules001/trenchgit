# Known Risks

#### Volatility

Crypto markets move quickly.

* Prices can change rapidly
* Opportunities and risks both appear fast

***

#### Slippage

Trades may execute at slightly different prices than expected.

* More common during fast market movement
* Can increase with larger trade sizes

***

#### Liquidity

Some tokens may have limited liquidity.

* Large orders can impact price
* Entry and exit may not always be smooth

***

#### No Guarantee of Profit

Data and signals are meant to assist, not predict.

* Market outcomes are uncertain
* All trading decisions are made by the user

***

#### Security Principles

* Verify transactions before signing
* Avoid unknown links or approvals
* Never share private keys
